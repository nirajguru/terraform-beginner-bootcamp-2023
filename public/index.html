Paneer Masala Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 lb paneer, cubed
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 4 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Chopped cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan on medium heat.
  2. Add onions and sauté until translucent.
  3. Add ginger, garlic and green chilies. Sauté for 1 minute.
  4. Add tomatoes and spices. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
  5. Add paneer cubes and mix well.
  6. Simmer for 5 minutes until paneer is cooked through.
  7. Garnish with cilantro and serve hot.
