Masala Dosa

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 cup urad dal
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Oil

For the masala filling:

  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Spices

Instructions

  1. Soak rice and urad dal overnight
  2. Grind to a smooth batter adding water as needed
  3. Leave batter to ferment overnight
  4. Make masala filling with potatoes, onions, tomatoes and spices
  5. Heat a dosa pan, add a thin layer of oil
  6. Pour batter and spread thinly to make dosa
  7. Cook dosa until crisp on both sides
  8. Add masala filling and serve hot
