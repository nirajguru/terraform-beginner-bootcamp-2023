Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients

  • 1 lb paneer, cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup cream
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a pan. Add cumin and coriander seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add onions and saute until translucent.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and saute for 1 min.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until mushy.
  5. Add turmeric, garam masala, salt. Mix well.
  6. Add paneer cubes. Cook for 2-3 mins.
  7. Add cream and mix well. Simmer for 2-3 mins.
  8. Garnish with cilantro and serve hot.
